Pineapple Mint Popsicles


Recipe by Cookie and Kate

Description

These Pineapple Mint Popsicles will be the star of your summer with how easy and tasty they are.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound frozen pineapple chunks, defrosted completely in the refrigerator
  • 1 tablespoon lightly packed fresh mint leaves, to taste

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine the thawed pineapple and mint. Blend until completely smooth. Taste, and add a few more mint leaves if youâ€™d prefer more minty flavor. Blend again.

  2. Pour the mixture into your popsicle molds, insert popsicle sticks. Freeze until frozen solid. Enjoy!
